Frequently Asked Questions

What bookkeeping services do you offer? ▼

We provide comprehensive bookkeeping services including data entry, bank reconciliation, accounts payable/receivable management, BAS preparation and lodgement, monthly financial reports, and more.

How does your payroll service work? ▼

Our payroll service includes employee setup, weekly/fortnightly/monthly pay runs, Single Touch Payroll (STP) lodgement, leave tracking, superannuation management, and year-end reconciliations at $680 + GST per employee annually.

Do you work with tax agents and accountants? ▼

Yes! We collaborate closely with registered tax agents and accountants across Australia, providing back-office support and ensuring all reporting meets professional standards.

What makes EDULINK different from competitors? ▼

We offer transparent pricing with no hidden costs, dedicated expertise working with registered professionals, and a focus on time and cost savings so you can focus on growing your business.
